Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours? +

Our delivery service operates from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM on weekdays, and extends from 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM on weekends.

How long does delivery typically take? +

Delivery times vary based on distance and traffic conditions, but typically range from 30-50 minutes.

Is there really free delivery for orders over RM100? +

Yes! We offer completely free delivery for all orders of RM100 and above, regardless of your location within our delivery zones. For orders below RM100, delivery fees are capped at a maximum of RM10. The free delivery applies automatically at checkout for qualifying orders.

Can I schedule a delivery for later? +

Yes! We offer pre-orders with delivery scheduled 3 days after you place your order. This is perfect for planning office meetings or ensuring your morning coffee is ready when you need it..
